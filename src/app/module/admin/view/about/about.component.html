The name indicates itself, EngFlexy is a compound word of two words Eng and Flexy. Eng is an acronym for English, and Flexy is an acronym for flexible. The basic idea here is that our platform must be far away from the traditional language centers (a building with teachers + stacked departments + a blackboard), using advanced technology. In fact, we have developed an interactive platform that is 100% online, that means, the learner is the one who chooses the appropriate time and place for him. Moreover, there is the possibility to choose between individual or group classes institution that inspires.
